          This is a list of all modifiable weapons, complete with information on available attachments and stat changes.

          For detailed descriptions of each statistic, see Weapon Mechanics.

          Mods inside a single category are sorted from highest to lowest in the leftmost applicable column (first by highest damage, then by highest accuracy, etc).

          Each weapon can have no more than one attachment from the same category at the same time. Mods from different categories are compatible unless they have the same number next to them in "conflict" column.

          LegendEdit

          • First row of the numbers (without "+" or "-") represent unmodified stats of the weapon.
          • Leftmost column contains attachment categories.
          • Second column contains names of the mods themselves.
          • Conflict column marks incompatible mods.
          Ammo = base total ammo capacity for the weapon
          Mag = magazine capacity
          Dmg = damage per round
          Acc = accuracy
          Stb = stability
          Con = concealment
          Thr = threat
          RoF = rate of fire, in rounds per minute
